About

Jenna Valentine is a graphic designer and multi-media artist based in Phoenix, Arizona. Growing up spending a lot of time outside, as well as a year working and living in Yosemite National Park, Jenna's life is heavily influenced by nature. This is reflected in her art and often comes as the main inspiration in her work.
